## Why this matters

A well-planned retirement transition ensures financial security across a multi-decade horizon while optimizing for tax efficiency, longevity risk, and spousal security. The decision to delay Social Security has lifetime impact on household cashflow. Failing to coordinate spousal benefits leaves money on the table.

## Definition of done

- Social Security claiming ages decided for both partners
- Tax-efficient withdrawal strategy documented
- Transition timeline from full-time to part-time to retirement explicitly defined
- Healthcare plan (Medicare enrollment timing, supplemental coverage) confirmed
- State residency decision finalized for tax purposes
- Annual income projections validated against actual Social Security statements
- Spousal benefit optimization confirmed through Social Security Administration
